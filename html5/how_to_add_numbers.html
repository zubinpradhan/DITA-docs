How to add numbers

Continue reading below to learn how to use the Numbers app to add two numbers.

To successfully add numbers, you must know the basics of mathematical operations.

Follow the steps below to find the sum of two numbers:

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Tap the plus (+) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Tap the equals (=) button.
The result of the addition is displayed.

Lets say you want to find the sum of 6 and 2. To do so, the mathematical operation will look like this: 6+2=8. Using the app, enter 6 followed by the plus button. Next, enter 2. Press the equals button.

The result "8" is displayed on the screen.

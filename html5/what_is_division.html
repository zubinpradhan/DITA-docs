What is Division?

This topic introduces the concept of division.

Division is usually denoted by 'รท' and is the opposite of multiplication. It involves two elements, the dividend and the divisor, where the dividend is divided by the divisor to give a single number called the quotient.

Rules of division

  • The division of two positive numbers is a positive number
  • The division of two negative numbers is a positive number
  • The division of numbers with different signs results in a negative number

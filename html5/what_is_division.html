What is division?

This topic introduces the concept of division.

Division is usually denoted by 'รท' and is the inverse of multiplication. It constitutes of two terms, dividend and divisor, where the dividend is divided by the divisor to give a single term value. When the dividend is greater than the divisor, the result obtained is greater than 1, or else it would be less than 1.

Rules of division

  • The division of two positive integers is a positive integer
  • The division of two negative number is a positive integer
  • The division of integers with different signs results in a negative integer.

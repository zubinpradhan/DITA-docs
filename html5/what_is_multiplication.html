What is Multiplication?

This topic introduces the concept of multiplication.

Multiplication is repeated addition. It denoted by ‘×’. The multiplication process involves two elements - the multiplicand and the multiplier. The result of the multiplication of the multiplicand and the multiplier is called the product.

Rules of division

  • The product of two positive numbers is a positive number
  • The product of two negative numbers is a positive number
  • The product of a positive and a negative number is a negative number

What is Addition?

This topic introduces the concept of addition.

Addition is the mathematical process of summing numbers together. The addition process is denoted by the plus ‘+’ sign. It involves combining two or more numbers into a single sum. The addition process is commutative, which means the order of the numbers does not matter.

Rules of addition

  • The addition of two positive numbers reults in a positive number
  • The addition of two negative numbers results in a negative number
  • While adding positive and negative numbers, subtract the numbers and use the sign of the largest number
